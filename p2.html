TIM HORTONS HOCKEY HALL OF FAME SPECIAL EDITION

It was a proud moment in Canadian History when the renowned Hockey Hall of Fame and Tim Hortons Restaurants teamed up together to create a special edition location to honour the history of Canadian Hockey.

Located at 181 Bay street in the Brookfield place this Tim Hortons stands out as iconic jerseys and Hockey memorabilia are showcased throughout the restaurant. One feels as if you are in a museum, however, the urge to grab a coffee and wonder through the exhibit is truly unique.

NAGC partnered closely with the Architect and Design team of this location to ensure all the details were perfect. Various wall murals, customized glass display cases, special logo signs, and remaining true to the architectural detail of Brookfield place were all vital to success.

The result is an awesomely, unique experience visitors will find no place else.
